As a U.S. Peace Corps Volunteer in the Philippines I provided project assistance in municipal government environmental and planning offices of Culasi, Antique.

I carried out a USAID grant funded project to empower local communities in marine conservation. I organized trainings for fishery law enforcement teams and facilitated the procurement of supplies, including uniforms and patrol boats. To complement those activities I also organized environmental education programs and participatory local governance workshops.

I used Participatory Analysis for Community Action (PACA) to analyze social and resource issues, and contributed to the creation of five year development plans for six communities.

I collaborated with area NGOs and international organizations on projects such as local governance capacity building for improved resource management and livelihoods.

I integrated with my community and was able to bring strength to local initiatives by adding diversity, not overriding with a foreign perspective.
